Super Admin

    Full access to all pages (Overview, Appointments, Leads, Blog, Events, Newsletter, Reports, Settings).

    Can manage other users and assign roles.

Content Manager

    Access: Blog/Articles, Newsletter, Events.

    Purpose: Handles publishing, editing, and scheduling content.

Sales Agent

    Access: Leads, Appointments, Overview.

    Purpose: Manages client leads and schedules meetings.

Analyst

    Access: Reports, Overview.

    Purpose: Reviews performance metrics and generates insights.

Event Coordinator

    Access: Events, Appointments.

    Purpose: Organizes and tracks event-related activities.

Basic User

    Access: Overview only.

    Purpose: Read-only access for general visibility.

System Admin

    Access: Settings, Overview.

    Purpose: Manages technical configurations but not business data.
